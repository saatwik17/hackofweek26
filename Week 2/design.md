# Restaurant Menu Website - Design Documentation

## Design Philosophy

### Color Palette
- **Primary Colors**: Deep amber (#D4A574), warm terracotta (#B85450), rich burgundy (#8B2635)
- **Secondary Colors**: Cream white (#FAF7F2), charcoal gray (#2C2C2C), golden yellow (#F4C430)
- **Accent Colors**: Fresh green (#4A7C59) for vegetarian items, spicy red (#C41E3A) for non-vegetarian

### Typography
- **Display Font**: "Playfair Display" - Bold serif for headings and hero text
- **Body Font**: "Inter" - Clean sans-serif for menu items and descriptions
- **Accent Font**: "Oswald" - Condensed sans-serif for prices and labels

### Visual Language
- **Modern Editorial Style**: Inspired by high-end food magazines and contemporary restaurant branding
- **Warm & Inviting**: Earthy tones that evoke comfort and appetite
- **Professional Clean**: Minimal design with strategic use of white space
- **Appetite Appeal**: Colors and imagery designed to stimulate hunger and craving

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Smooth animations for menu item reveals and cart interactions
- **Splide.js**: Hero image carousel and featured items slider
- **ECharts.js**: Data visualization for popular items and ratings
- **Typed.js**: Typewriter effect for hero tagline
- **Splitting.js**: Text animation effects for section headings
- **p5.js**: Background particle effects and interactive elements

### Animation & Effects
- **Hero Section**: Typewriter animation for main tagline with color cycling
- **Menu Cards**: Staggered reveal animation on scroll using Anime.js
- **Category Filter**: Smooth transition effects with opacity and transform
- **Cart Interactions**: Bounce and scale animations when adding items
- **Background**: Subtle floating food particle effects using p5.js
- **Scroll Motion**: Gentle reveal animations (16px movement, 200ms duration)

### Header Effect
- **Navigation Bar**: Fixed header with subtle shadow on scroll
- **Hero Background**: Animated gradient overlay with food imagery
- **Brand Logo**: Animated entrance with scale and fade effects

### Interactive Elements
- **Menu Cards**: Hover effects with 3D tilt and shadow expansion
- **Category Buttons**: Color morphing and scale transforms on active state
- **Cart Icon**: Pulsing animation when items are added
- **Price Tags**: Golden highlight animation on hover
- **Add to Cart**: Success animation with checkmark reveal

### Layout & Grid
- **Responsive Grid**: CSS Grid with Tailwind classes for menu items
- **Card Design**: Clean white cards with subtle shadows and rounded corners
- **Image Treatment**: High-quality food photography with consistent aspect ratios
- **Typography Hierarchy**: Clear visual hierarchy with proper spacing

### Color Usage
- **Vegetarian Items**: Green accent borders and labels
- **Non-Vegetarian Items**: Red accent borders and labels
- **Popular Items**: Golden badge with star icon
- **New Items**: Orange "New" badge with pulse animation
- **Price Display**: Bold golden color with rupee symbol

### Background & Atmosphere
- **Consistent Background**: Warm cream color (#FAF7F2) throughout
- **Decorative Elements**: Subtle food-themed patterns and shapes
- **Section Dividers**: Animated dividers with food icons
- **Loading States**: Skeleton screens with warm color placeholders

This design creates a sophisticated yet approachable restaurant menu experience that rivals major fast food chains while maintaining a unique, modern aesthetic that appeals to contemporary diners.
